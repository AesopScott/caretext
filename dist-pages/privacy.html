Care Text Privacy.

Last updated June 18, 2026. This policy explains what Care Text collects, why we collect it, and how we use it to support subscriber-directed encouragement messages.

Information We Collect

We may collect subscriber account information such as name, email address, login details, subscription status, and billing plan. Subscribers may enter connection information such as a recipient name, phone number, relationship type, sender name, selected message categories, and delivery frequency.

Message and Consent Information

To operate SMS delivery responsibly, we may store opt-in and opt-out records, including phone number, subscriber relationship, consent status, opt-in timestamp, opt-out timestamp, HELP or STOP requests, and related delivery logs.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide the Care Text service, manage subscriber accounts, generate or prepare encouragement messages, send opt-in invitations, deliver messages after recipient consent, respect opt-outs, provide support, prevent abuse, and improve the product.

AI-Assisted Messages

Care Text may use AI-assisted tools to help create or shape messages based on selected categories, relationship settings, and subscriber preferences. Subscribers should review their settings and use Care Text only for appropriate personal connection.

How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help us operate Care Text, such as hosting providers, authentication providers, payment processors, SMS providers, email providers, analytics tools, and security services. We may also disclose information when required by law or to protect the service from abuse.

SMS Choices

Recipients can opt out of SMS messages by replying STOP. Recipients can reply HELP for help. Once a phone number opts out, Care Text stops sending ongoing SMS messages to that number unless a valid new opt-in is completed.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. No internet service is perfectly secure, and subscribers should avoid entering sensitive medical, financial, legal, or emergency information into Care Text.

Children

Care Text is not intended for children under 13. Subscribers should only add recipients when they have the right and relationship context to invite that person to receive messages.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@caretext.net.
